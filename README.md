# ☕ Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 🗂️ Overview
A data-driven Excel dashboard that transforms raw coffee sales data into actionable insights.
This project analyzes sales performance across multiple dimensions — including time, roast type,
size, geography, and customer loyalty — delivering a clear and interactive view of business trends
for smarter decision-making.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📈 **Sales Trend Analysis** — Line charts tracking total sales over time, revealing seasonal
  fluctuations, growth patterns, and performance shifts across different periods.
- 👑 **Top Customers** — Bar charts ranking the highest-value customers by sales volume,
  enabling targeted marketing and stronger customer relationship management.
- 🌍 **Top Countries** — Bar charts identifying the leading countries by sales contribution,
  supporting geographic market strategy and expansion planning.

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Dimensions Covered

- 🗓️ **Time** — Sales trends tracked across months and years
- 🔥 **Roast Type** — Performance breakdown by coffee roast (Light, Medium, Dark)
- 📏 **Size** — Sales analysis by product size variants
- 🌐 **Country** — Geographic distribution of sales volume
- 🎖️ **Customer Loyalty** — Insights segmented by loyalty card holders vs. non-members

---

## 🎯 Goals

- ✅ Uncover deeper trends and patterns within coffee sales data.
- ✅ Identify the key drivers behind sales performance.
- ✅ Support data-driven decisions across product development, pricing, and marketing.
- ✅ Provide stakeholders with a clear, concise, and shared view of business performance.

## 📊 Key Metrics Covered

| 📌 Metric | 📝 Description |
|-----------|---------------|
| ☕ Total Sales | Overall revenue generated across all coffee products |
| 👑 Top Customers | Highest contributing customers ranked by sales volume |
| 🌍 Top Countries | Leading markets ranked by total sales contribution |
| 🔥 Roast Performance | Sales breakdown across Light, Medium, and Dark roast types |
| 🎖️ Loyalty Insights | Sales comparison between loyalty card and non-loyalty customers |

---

## 🏆 Project Outcome

The Coffee Sales Dashboard delivers meaningful insights into sales performance across all key
business dimensions. By consolidating data into a single interactive view, it equips stakeholders
with the clarity needed to make confident, evidence-based decisions — from refining product
offerings to targeting high-value customers and markets.
